AOA New York is an association of private dealers and galleries specializing in antique material from Africa, Oceania and the Americas. AOA New York is committed to presenting only the best of what the tribal market has to offer as we return for our second year. The 2012 fair will take place in Fletcher-Sinclair mansion and in participating galleries in the Madison Avenue area.

Please join us for an exciting weekend dedicated to the arts of Africa, Oceania and the Americas.
